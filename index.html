About the Residence

A sanctuary of comfort in the Val di Cornia

Poggio Agliai is a 19th-century farmhouse lovingly renovated to honour its Tuscan heritage while offering every modern comfort. Four spacious ensuite bedrooms sleep up to eight guests, while generous living areas open onto pergola-shaded terraces and a glistening saltwater pool. An aromatic herb garden, private olive grove, and panoramic views of the rolling countryside set the stage for la dolce vita.

Spend your days exploring nearby Suvereto, Bolgheri, and the Etruscan Coast, or indulge in private wine tastings and chef-prepared meals at home. As evening falls, the warm glow of terracotta tiles and stone walls creates an atmosphere of intimate serenity.

Highlights

Experiences

Curated moments tailored to you

Let us craft an itinerary that balances exploration and relaxation, ensuring your time at Poggio Agliai feels effortlessly indulgent.
